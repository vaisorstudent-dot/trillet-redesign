Common questions

What agencies ask before signing up.

"What if my clients don't like the AI?"

Every agent is customizable — tone, personality, instructions, escalation rules. If a client wants changes, you adjust in minutes. No engineering tickets. And the AI improves continuously from call data.

"Can I really invoice clients through the platform?"

Yes. Connect your Stripe. Set any price per client. Trillet bills them automatically under your brand. They never see Trillet. You keep 100% of the spread above your platform cost.

"How is this different from just reselling Retell or Vapi?"

Those are developer platforms — they give you an API and expect you to build the product. Trillet IS the product. White-label, rebilling, sub-accounts, agent builder — all built in. You don't need a single engineer.

"What if I only have a few clients to start?"

Start with Agency at $299/mo. Even 2-3 clients at $299/ea covers your cost and puts you in profit. As you grow, upgrade to Lite SaaS for template deployment, or Pro for full vertical distribution.

"Do my clients know it's Trillet underneath?"

No. Full white-label means your brand everywhere — login page, dashboard, emails, invoices, even the domain. One of our agencies has 47 clients and none of them know Trillet exists.
