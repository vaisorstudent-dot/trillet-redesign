## Case Study 1: National Legal Services Hotline (Government)

**Vertical:** Government / Legal
**Deployment:** Full production — all enquiries and paralegal intake

**Challenge:**
- Missing up to 100 calls daily during peak periods
- No coverage for after-hours urgent enquiries
- Staff overwhelmed with repetitive intake questions

**Results:**
- 100% call coverage — zero missed calls
- Instant response — no hold times
- 10+ concurrent calls handled simultaneously
- FAQs automated, intake for new clients, immediate escalation during emergencies

**Implementation:** 4-month proof-of-concept to full deployment

## Case Study 2: Financial Services — Debt Collection (Enterprise)

**Vertical:** Financial services / Debt collection
**Deployment:** 24/7 AI agents handling all inbound collection calls

**Challenge:**
- 50,000+ inbound calls/month overwhelming agents
- Missing after-hours collection opportunities
- High operational costs, long wait times

**Results (Jan–May 2025):**
- **$338,357+ collected** through AI-handled calls
- **0.89% call abandonment rate** (industry average: 8–10%) — **~10x better**
- Peak month: **$161,976 collected** (April 2025)
- Secure payment processing + user verification
- Real-time CRM updates for every interaction
- Warm transfers with full context when needed

**Implementation:** 3 months
