### Trillet's Addressable Market (SAM/SOM)
The TAM is broad (all voice AI). Trillet's SAM is narrower — specifically:

**SAM = Voice AI for SMB/agency deployment**
- ~33M small businesses in the US alone
- ~6M use service-based models where inbound calls = revenue (trades, legal, medical, real estate)
- At $59/mo (D2C) or $299/mo (agency managing 10-50), conservative SAM: **$2–5B**

**SOM = Agency-deployable voice AI platforms (Trillet's actual competitive space)**
- This category essentially doesn't exist yet (per Ori's insight)
- GHL's agency segment provides the closest proxy: 2M+ businesses on platform, $297–497/mo agency tiers
- Trillet's realistic 12-month SOM: capture early agency adopters in the greenfield
- **First-mover in a category is the SOM story — not market share capture**

> **Investor framing**: "The voice AI market is $6.8B today, growing to $32.6B. But nobody has built the GHL for it — the agency platform layer. We have."

## 3. Competitive Landscape

### Developer Platforms (build-your-own voice AI)

| Company | Funding | Valuation | Stage | Positioning |
|---------|---------|-----------|-------|-------------|
| **Bland AI** | $65M total ($40M Series B, Feb 2025) | Not disclosed | Growth | Outbound call automation at scale. Enterprise. |
| **Vapi** | $22.1M ($20M Series A, Dec 2024) | ~$130M post-money | Series A | Developer-first infrastructure. Maximum customization (4.2K config points). |
| **Synthflow** | $30M ($20M Series A, Jun 2025) | Not disclosed | Series A | No-code platform. Deeper workflow customization. Global expansion. |
| **Retell AI** | $5.1M (Seed, Aug 2024) | Not disclosed | Seed | Developer-first. Fastest/most natural conversations. Easiest to use. |

### Where Trillet Is Different (the investor pitch)

**None of these are Trillet's actual competitors.** They all solve a different problem:

| | Dev Platforms (Retell, Vapi, etc.) | Trillet |
|---|---|---|
| **Buyer** | Developers / technical teams | Agencies / non-technical resellers |
| **Deployment** | Build from scratch per use case | Auto-configure from URL in 5 min |
| **Business model** | Usage-based API pricing | Platform subscription + white-label |
| **White-label** | DIY (you build the wrapper) | Built-in (your brand, domain, rebilling) |
| **Lock-in** | Code-level (API dependency) | Operational (calls go unanswered if you leave) |
| **Template/scaling** | Not applicable | Template library + one-click deploy |

> **Investor framing**: "Retell and Vapi raised $70M+ combined to let developers *build* voice AI. We let agencies *sell* it. We're not competing with them — we're their distribution layer."
