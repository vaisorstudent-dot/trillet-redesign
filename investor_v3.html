Defensibility

Four structural advantages

01

Telephony exclusivity

You can A/B test email tools. You can run parallel chatbots. You cannot run two AI receptionists on the same phone number. One number, one system. Winner-take-all at the endpoint — by physics, not by contract.

At 50 endpoints: painful to switch. At 500: effectively permanent.
02

Regulated industry beachhead

Government, financial services, European banking — deployed and running. Competitors start with restaurants and spas. We started at the top.

Speed to deployment = speed to market = speed to stickiness.

First-mover advantage in how the next generation discovers software.
