FAQ

Common questions.

Can I upgrade between plans?

Yes. Move from Starter to Agency to Lite SaaS at any time. Your agents, clients, and configuration carry over. Downgrading works too — no lock-in.

What are usage costs on top of the base price?

You pay per minute of AI call time. Rates vary by region and language. Most US calls are $0.08–0.12/min. Agency plans can mark up usage and rebill to clients.

Do clients see the Trillet brand?

Not on Agency plans and above. Full white-label means your brand, your domain, your login. Clients interact only with your brand.

How does rebilling work?

Set your own price per client. Connect your Stripe. Trillet bills your clients automatically under your brand. You keep 100% of the spread above your Trillet cost.

What's the difference between Agency and Lite SaaS?

Deployment model. On Agency, you manually configure each agent per client — full control, more hands-on. On Lite SaaS, you pick a template, enter a client's URL, and the agent deploys in one click. Lite SaaS also adds client self-onboarding, so they can sign up without you touching anything.

What's included in the $14K Pro SaaS template build?

We build a custom voice AI agent designed specifically for your vertical — workflows, terminology, compliance, integrations. That agent becomes a deployable template exclusive to your network. Build takes ~2 weeks. You deploy it to unlimited endpoints via API.
